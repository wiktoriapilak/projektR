Description: This dataset contains data on the popularity of various search terms on Google Trends over time.

Format: CSV (Comma-Separated Values)

Data Structure: Each CSV file in this dataset follows a similar structure:

Column 1: Month - the month and year of the data point (string)
Column 2: Search Interest - the search interest score for the corresponding search term (integer)

License: The data is available under the terms of the Open Data Commons Open Database License (ODbL).
